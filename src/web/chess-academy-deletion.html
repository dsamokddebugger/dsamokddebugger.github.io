Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

At IdeaSpace, we are committed to giving you full control over your personal data. In compliance with the Google Play Developer Policies, this page details the steps to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from our Chess Academy mobile application.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Fill out the Data Deletion Request Form below.
  2. Click the Submit Deletion Request button.
  3. This will automatically draft a pre-formatted email in your default mail application.
  4. Send the email to finalize your request. We will verify and process your request within 7 business days.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon requesting account deletion, all data tied to your profile is permanently erased. This includes:

  • Personal Identity Data: Your name, registered email address, and profile picture URL associated with your Google Account sign-in.
  • Gameplay & Progress Data: Your Chess ELO ratings, puzzles completed, match history logs, daily assignments progress, and achievements.
  • App Preferences: Selected board styles, piece sets, sound volume configurations, and custom chess engine settings.

What Data is Kept?

  • No Personal Identifiers Kept: All links between your identity and application logs are severed. We do not retain email addresses or names.
  • Anonymized Match Data: In order to maintain rating balance calculations and general game analytics, aggregate anonymous game metadata (e.g., moves played in a game) may be kept, completely detached from any specific user profile or identifier.

Data Retention Period

  • Active Database deletion: Deletion requests made via the web form or email are verified and processed within 7 business days.
  • Backup Systems: Remnants of deleted data may persist in our secure, encrypted database backups for up to 30 days, after which they are completely overwritten.
