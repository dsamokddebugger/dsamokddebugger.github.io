Privacy Policy for Nu: Simple Calculator

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

At Nu: Simple Calculator, we prioritize your privacy and data security. This document details how we handle information in the Nu mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Nu is designed as a local utility tool. We understand that your calculations are private.

1.1 No Personal Information

We do not require users to provide any personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone number. The app is fully functional without any account registration.

1.2 Local Processing

All mathematical inputs and results are processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any of the numbers you enter or the results of your calculations.

2. Third-Party Services

For app maintenance and to provide a smooth user experience, we use third-party services that may collect information for diagnostic or advertising purposes:

  • Google Play Services: Ensures app security and distribution.
  • AdMob: Displays ads to support free development. They may use anonymous device IDs for personalization.
  • Log Data: In the event of an app crash, we may collect technical data (device type, OS version, app state) through third-party tools to identify and fix bugs. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

3. Permissions

Nu requests minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Necessary for loading ad content and internal framework updates.
  • Vibration Control: For haptic feedback (if enabled in settings).

We do not request access to your contacts, photos, camera, or location.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our application is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here and are effective immediately upon publication.
