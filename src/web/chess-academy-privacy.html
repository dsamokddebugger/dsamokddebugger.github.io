Privacy Policy for IdeaSpace Chess Academy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

IdeaSpace Chess Academy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App"), in full compliance with the Google Play Developer Policies.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a secure, synchronized, and engaging chess-learning experience, we collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Data

  • Authentication Details: If you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture. If you choose to use the App in "Anonymous" mode, no personal authentication details are collected.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us for customer support, we may collect your email address and any message details you provide.

B. Gameplay & Progress Data

  • User Statistics: We collect ELO ratings, puzzle completion rates, match history, and daily assignment progress.
  • Game Configurations: We store preferences such as board theme selections, piece sets, audio volume configurations, and Stockfish engine limits.

C. Technical Data

  • Device & OS Information: To ensure compatibility and performance, we may log device specifications (e.g., OS version, CPU architecture) for local performance optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your personal chess profile.
  • Synchronize your ratings, game logs, and unlocked store items across multiple devices.
  • Track and display your cognitive tactical analytics on the Dashboard radar chart.
  • Provide responsive support and process troubleshooting requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure: Your synchronized game profiles, analytics, and authentication tokens are managed securely through Firebase Cloud Services.
  • On-Device Storage: Offline configurations and local engine game history are stored locally on your device (using secure shared preferences and local databases).
  • Data Encryption: All communication between the App and Firebase/external servers is protected using industry-standard Secure Socket Layer/Transport Layer Security (SSL/TLS) encryption.

4. Third-Party Sharing and Advertisements

  • No Advertising Networks: We do not partner with, distribute data to, or embed SDKs from third-party advertising brokers. Your profile details are strictly confidential.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.
  • Service Providers: We only use Google Firebase services to manage secure authentication and cloud database synchronization.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed to offer a safe educational environment for all chess enthusiasts. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us, and we will take immediate action to delete that data.

6. Data Control and Deletion Rights

You have complete control over your personal data. You may:

  • Request an export of your collected chess profile data.
  • Request the complete and permanent deletion of your authentication account, ratings, and match history.

To exercise these rights, you can submit an online request via our dedicated Account & Data Deletion Page or email us directly at apps@ideaspaceapps.store. We will process your deletion request within 7 business days.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be made available inside the App's "About Us" tab and updated on our store page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Developer Email: apps@ideaspaceapps.store
  • Official Website: https://ideaspaceapps.store
