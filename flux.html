The FLUX Guide: How to Use

Using FLUX is intuitive. Follow this simple flow to map out your financial journey:

  • 1
    Initial Investment

    Adjust the slider to reflect your current starting capital.

  • 2
    Monthly Deposit

    Set your recurring contribution to see the power of consistency.

  • 3
    Annual Return

    Enter your expected yearly ROI (Return on Investment).

  • 4
    Inflation Rate

    Input the projected inflation rate (e.g., 3–4%) to see real-world value.

  • 5
    Lifestyle Expense

    Enter current cost of living; FLUX projects the future adjusted cost.

  • 6
    Time & Mode

    Select horizon (up to 50 years) and toggle between Simple/Compound modes.

📚 Glossary of Terms

Inflation-Adjusted Value

The value of your future wealth expressed in today's purchasing power.

Future Value

The total nominal amount of money you will have at the end of the term.

Months of Support

How many months you can live off your savings, based on projected expenses.

Future Cost

An inflation-adjusted projection of what your current lifestyle will cost.

Total Invested

The total principal (Initial + Monthly Deposits) you have contributed.

Interest Earned

The total profit generated by your investment over time.

Real Growth

The net percentage increase in your wealth after inflation and deposits.

Compounding

The process where interest is earned on both principal and accumulated interest.
