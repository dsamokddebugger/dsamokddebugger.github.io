Privacy Policy for IdeaSpace Applications

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

At IdeaSpace, we are committed to providing powerful utility tools while ensuring your digital privacy is respected and protected. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed by IdeaSpace, including but not limited to Nu, Flux, Amber, Omni, and Kingslayer.

By using our applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We believe in data minimalism. Our applications are designed to function primarily as local utilities.

1.1 Personal Data

Unlike many other apps, we do not personally collect, store, or sell any of your private data on our own servers. Our apps do not require you to create an account or provide identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number to access core features.

1.2 Local Processing

All calculations, financial projections, or personal settings you enter within our apps are processed exclusively on your device. This data remains private to you and is not transmitted to us.

2. Third-Party Services

To support the development and maintenance of our free applications, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for advertising or analytics purposes.

These services include:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and security.
  • AdMob: To display advertisements. They may use your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads.
  • Firebase Analytics: To help us understand how users interact with our apps so we can improve the experience.

3. Permissions

Our applications may request basic permissions necessary for functionality:

  • Internet Access: Required for displaying advertisements and core framework updates.
  • Device Storage: Only if a specific feature requires saving data locally on your device.

We do not access your contacts, photos, or location unless a feature explicitly requires it and you have granted permission.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
