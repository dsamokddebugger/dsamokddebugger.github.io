Privacy Policy Report: AMBER Sudoku

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

This report outlines the privacy-related aspects of the AMBER Sudoku application. It is intended to assist in the creation of a formal Privacy Policy for app store submissions (Google Play Store, Apple App Store).

1. Overview

AMBER Sudoku is a mobile application developed using Flutter. It is a puzzle game that operates primarily offline, with minimal network engagement for non-core features (ads and fonts).

2. Data Collection

2.1 Personal Data

The app does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly from the user.

  • No user accounts or registrations are required.
  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses are requested or stored.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

The app uses third-party services that may collect data automatically:

Service Purpose Data Collected (Approximate)
Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) Monetization Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA), device info, coarse location, usage data.
Google Fonts Typography IP address (for font delivery).

3. Data Storage (Local)

The app stores data locally on the user's device using SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to any external servers by the app.

  • Game Progress: Current grid state, solution, move history, and timer.
  • User Profiles: In-game nicknames (e.g., "Leo", "Maya"), levels, XP, and stats.
  • Settings: Theme preferences, sound settings, and ad-free status.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access (android.permission.INTERNET): Required to load advertisements and fetch Google Fonts.

5. Third-Party Services

The following third-party libraries are integrated:

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK: Used to display banner and interstitial ads.
  • Google Fonts: Used for custom typography.

6. Recommendations for Formal Privacy Policy

When drafting your formal privacy policy, ensure you:

  • Link to Third-Party Policies: Include links to the privacy policies of Google Play Services and AdMob.
  • Disclosure of Local Storage: State that the app stores game data locally on the device.
  • Target Audience: If the app is targeted at children, ensure compliance with COPPA (US) or GDPR-K (EU). Sudoku is generally considered for all ages, but the "Leo/Maya" characters might appeal to children.
  • Opt-Out: Mention that users can reset their Advertising ID in their device settings to limit personalized ads.
